About

Sta. Elena High School has served the community since 19XX. We provide a balanced education that nurtures academic growth, character formation, and civic responsibility. Our faculty and staff are dedicated to helping each student reach their full potential.

Students are encouraged to participate in co-curricular activities, community service, and leadership programs that complement classroom learning.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide students with quality basic education and the necessary knowledge and skills on Information Technology in their preparation for global competition.

Vision

Sta. Elena High School, a learning institution commited to the culture of excellence, discipline and good taste, aims to produce funtionally literate students who are ready for global citizenship.

Academic Programs

  • Junior High School — Grades 7 to 10
  • Senior High School — Academic and Technical-Vocational tracks
  • Special Programs — Remedial classes, enrichment clubs, student leadership

Facilities

  • Science and computer laboratories
  • Multi-purpose hall and covered court
  • Guidance and counseling center
